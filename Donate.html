Donate to Weber County Search & Rescue

Your Donation is Greatly Appreciated

Your donation to Weber County Search and Rescue will be used to fund critical training for members, life saving equipment and education and outreach to the Weber County community. You can donate online or by mail. All donations are tax deductible.

-OR-

You can certainly make an offline donation by mailing a check payable to “Weber County Search & Rescue” On the memo line of the check, please indicate that the donation is for “WeberSAR Drone.” Please mail your check to:

Weber County Search & Rescue
721 W 12th St.
Ogden, UT 84404

Thank you!
